# My take on animal rights


Having learned about the history of the animal protection movement, and its expression in demands for animal rights and for improved welfare, discuss where you stand on the issue. Do you take an animal rights stance, or are you more in favor of improving animal welfare?

------

Personally I am in favor of improving animal welfare, and the animal rights stance is much too extreme in my opinion. As of now humans need animals and their usage, getting rid of all animal products, all working animals, all domestic animals, etc. is simply not possible given how much humans survival depends on those things. Instead of getting rid of such things all together we should minimize the amount of suffering they cause.
