# Pet cost comparison

Compare the costs of having a pet from your worksheet with the results of one other student. In particular, see whether you overlooked any expenses this student raised or whether costs differ in different geographical areas.


--------

While many think owning a pet is cheap, there are many hidden costs no one thinks about. Adopting a pet as a member of the family is exciting, however one must first consider how capable they are of properly caring for a pet financially. This complete worksheet shows the average prices of nearly everything the average pet owner will have to consider.
