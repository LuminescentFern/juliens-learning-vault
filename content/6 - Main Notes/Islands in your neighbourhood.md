# Islands in your neighbourhood

Think about where you are right now. Outside your window, down the street, or even inside a building you are sitting in.

Imagine you are a plant, or an insect, or a small animal. What sort of "islands" might be in your surrounding environment that you don't even think about? How might you experience this landscape as islands if you were a different organism.

Describe your surroundings and how other organisms might see them as an "island" or a group of islands.

-----

I live near a marsh that has a river cutting through it. The marsh is surrounded by busy streets, houses and businesses. Many animals make the marsh and river their home, such as ducks, fish and squirrels. The surrounding area outside the marsh would not be suitable for their survival. In this sense, the marsh is like an island for the species who live in it.
