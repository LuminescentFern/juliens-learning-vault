# You were given a unique superpower

This video by Leon Hendrix explores the concept of your "superpower"—the unique combination of your nature (how you naturally think, feel, and operate) and your early life experiences.

- **Defining Your Nature**: Your nature is your brain's "default setting." Successful people, from Elon Musk to MrBeast, are those who leverage this nature in their chosen field.
    
- **The "Zone of Excellence" Trap**: Hendrix warns against the "zone of excellence"—tasks you have learned to be very good at, but that are not inherently energizing. These "golden handcuffs" can distract you from your "zone of genius," the work that truly aligns with your nature.
    
- **How to Identify Your Superpower**:
    
    - **Track Energy**: Categorize tasks as draining, neutral, or energizing. Aim to maximize time spent on energizing activities.
        
    - **Ask Others**: Friends and family—especially those who knew you in childhood—can often spot your unique strengths better than you can.
        
    - **Identify Your "Unreasonable" Strengths**: Look for things that are obvious and easy for you, but seem bafflingly difficult for others.
        
- **Overcoming "Shoulds"**: A major barrier to embracing your superpower is the "should" trap—adopting cultural values or career paths that feel expected rather than authentic.
    
- **Finding the Right Vehicle**: Your nature is the "soil," and your passions are the "plants." You can change the vehicle (the job or industry) without changing your foundational nature. If something isn't working, often small shifts in the "how" can make a task align with your superpower.
    

This video reinforces the idea of working _with_ your natural wiring rather than fighting against it.
