# How pig parts make the world turn

The Project: Meindertsma tracked the various parts of one specific pig (designated "05049") to discover how many products it contributed to after its life.  

 The Findings: She discovered that parts of the pig were used in at least 185 to 187 different non-pork products.
   
 Diverse Applications: The range of products is astonishing, spanning from common household items to specialized medical and industrial materials.  
 
 Examples: These products include everything from bullets and industrial materials to artificial hearts.  
 
 Key Takeaway: The talk highlights how integrated and essential animal byproducts are in modern manufacturing and medicine, often in ways that are completely invisible to the average consumer
