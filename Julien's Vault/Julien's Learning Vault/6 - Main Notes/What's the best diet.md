# What's the best diet?

Vegetarian, vegan, pescatarian, flexitarian, omnivore, Paleo, Mediterranean, and more. These are just some of the diet options that are available today. Although each has advantages and disadvantages, is there one that you consider the best--for your health, for animals, and for the environment? If there is no clear answer, how would you decide which diet to adopt?

-------

Out of all of the options, Mediterranean is what I personally think is the best overall choice. It is a very balanced diet that has incredible health benefits such as providing strong heart health. The mediterranean diet includes small amounts of meat, focusing mainly on fish. The environmental impact is moderate at most, but closer to low. Overall Mediterranean is the best diet for health, it is much less harmful to animals than many other diets, and it doesn't have a large negative impact on the environment.
