# Do we need zoos

The justifications for zoos include conservation and education, but zoos also provide entertainment. Considering the evidence that most species suffer in captivity, should we still have zoos? Are education and conservation valid reasons to confine animals?

-------

I believe the best option would be to replace zoos with better alternatives, such as larger, conservation specific areas that are designed to more adequately meet the psychological and physical needs of the animals. Zoos as they are are too small, and the environment isn't providing the stimulus they need to keep their instincts intact.
