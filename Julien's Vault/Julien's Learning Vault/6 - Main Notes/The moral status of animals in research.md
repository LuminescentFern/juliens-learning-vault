# The moral status of animals in research

What is our moral obligation to the animals used in research? Do we have the same obligations to all species of animals, whether mice or primates?

-------- 

Our moral obligation to the animals used in research is to avoid unnecessary suffering the most we can. Although there are some tests we as of now have no other choice but to conduct on living creatures, we should always be on the lookout for alternatives. We have this obligation to any living being.
