# My experience with animals and emotions

Describe your personal experiences with animals and emotions. These could include direct experiences with animals of any species or indirect experiences, through print or online media.

----

A simple example is this; I was at a friends house where their friendly cat went up to me. I pet the cat and it purred, showing happiness. My friend had two other cats, one who was aggressive and rude, and another who was skittish. The aggressive one scratched whoever got near it, and hissed at people for food, however it wasn't aggressive at all to my friends baby brother and was quite fond of him. The skittish one was terrified of anyone new, and although they like me now, when I first met the cat it hid from me. I feel these three cats serve as a good example to how animals each have their own personalities and emotions.
