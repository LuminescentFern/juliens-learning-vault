# Media representation of animals in society

After completing this module, you should have an understanding of the symbolic roles of animals in society. In this discussion question, you will demonstrate your understanding using representation of animals in the media. Describe how animals work in representation using concepts from this module, such as:

• Using animals as a metaphor for an idea, object, or social problem;

• Using animals to “stand in” for humans, in either a positive or a negative way;

• Using animals to portray a group of people; or

• Using animals to racialize people because of their animal practices.


-----

Kelloggs Frosted flakes, a cereal brand uses a anthropomorphic Tiger to symbolize strength, as Tigers are known to be some of the most powerful predators in the animal kingdom. They use a Tiger out of all animals because their marketing and public image revolves heavily around Hockey, a sport that requires strength and fitness. Kelloggs Frosted flakes are marketed to be a nutritious and energizing breakfast.
